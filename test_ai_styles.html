Understanding Your Rights

  • Free Appropriate Public Education (FAPE) - Your child's right to a free education
  • Least Restrictive Environment (LRE) - Learning alongside peers when possible
  • Parent Participation - Your right to be involved in educational decisions
    • Request evaluations and assessments
    • Review and approve IEP goals
    • Dispute decisions through due process
  • Procedural Safeguards - Protection of your child's rights

Local Resources
